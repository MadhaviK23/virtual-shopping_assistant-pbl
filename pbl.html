AI-Powered Smart Shopping System

A privacy-preserving edge AI system integrating smart shopping assistance, product tracking, size estimation, and automated billing into one unified retail experience.

Problem Statement

In large retail environments such as malls and supermarkets, customers often face difficulties in locating products, receiving personalized assistance, and experiencing delays at checkout counters. Traditional retail systems rely heavily on human staff and manual billing, leading to inefficiencies and poor customer experience. There is a need for an intelligent, automated solution that can assist customers, track product activity, and simplify billing using AI and computer vision.

Market Research

Existing solutions include Amazon Go (AI checkout), smart mirrors (H&M, Nike, Burberry), and retail analytics cameras. Computer vision in retail exists but is used in isolated parts.

Innovation

Unified AI ecosystem combining shopping assistance, product tracking, body-size analysis, and billing automation in one system. Privacy-first edge processing ensures no cloud facial data storage.
